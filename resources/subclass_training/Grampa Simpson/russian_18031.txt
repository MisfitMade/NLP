One trick is to tell them stories that won't go away... Like the time I took the ferry to Shelbyville. I needed a new heel for my shoes, so I decided to go to Morganville, as Shelbyville was then called. So I tied an onion to my belt, which was fashionable at the time... now the ferry cost a nickel, and in those days there were images of bumblebees on nickels. “Give me five bees for a quarter,” you say. So where were we? Oh, yes, it was important that I had an onion on my belt, which was fashionable at that time. They didn't have white onions because of the war...
